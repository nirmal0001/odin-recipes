Chicken Alfredo

Description

This creamy Chicken Alfredo is a comforting dish with tender chicken breasts, a rich, velvety Alfredo sauce, and perfectly cooked fettuccine. It's a family favorite that is both filling and flavorful.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 (16 ounce) package fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat and cook chicken for 5-7 minutes per side, until golden brown and cooked through. Remove from heat and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, melt butter and stir in heavy cream. Bring to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese and garlic powder, and cook until the sauce thickens, about 3-5 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, cook fettuccine according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. Slice the chicken breasts and add them to the Alfredo sauce. Serve over the cooked fettuccine, garnished with chopped parsley if desired.
