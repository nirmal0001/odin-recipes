Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

This classic Italian spaghetti bolognese features a rich, meaty tomato sauce paired with perfectly cooked spaghetti. The sauce is slow-cooked with onions, garlic, and herbs for the best depth of flavor. A perfect family meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • Parmesan cheese, grated (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add onion and garlic, and cook until soft, about 3 minutes.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, red wine (if using), basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer on low for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. Serve the Bolognese sauce over the cooked spaghetti, topped with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
