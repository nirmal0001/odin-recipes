Veggie Stir-Fry

Description

This quick and easy veggie stir-fry is full of fresh, colorful vegetables stir-fried in a savory soy-based sauce. Perfect for a healthy lunch or dinner, this dish is light yet satisfying!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 cup snap peas
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add garlic and ginger and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  2. Add the bell peppers, zucchini, carrot, and snap peas. Stir-fry for about 5-7 minutes, until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
  3. Stir in soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and sesame oil. Continue cooking for another 2-3 minutes, making sure everything is well coated.
  4. Serve the stir-fried vegetables over cooked rice. Enjoy!
